Job Market Paper

Foreign Institutional Investors and Underperforming CEOs: Evidence from Chinese Listed Firms , with Martin Strieborny.
I study how foreign institutional investors affect the forced turnover of underperforming CEOs in Chinese listed firms, using the staggered opening of the Stock Connect program as a natural experiment. Distinguishing de jure from de facto foreign ownership, I show that the disciplining effect works primarily through improved price informativeness rather than direct governance pressure. In a horse race between the two channels, the price-informativeness channel dominates.
