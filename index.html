Job Market Paper

Foreign Institutional Investors and Underperforming CEOs: Evidence from Chinese Listed Firms , with Martin Strieborny.
This paper studies how foreign institutional investors affect the forced turnover of underperforming CEOs in Chinese listed firms, exploiting the staggered opening of the Stock Connect program as a natural experiment. Distinguishing de jure from de facto foreign ownership, I find that the disciplining effect operates mainly through improved price informativeness rather than direct governance, with the price-informativeness channel dominating in a horse race between the two.
